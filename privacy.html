Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At WealthBuilders, we are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we collect, use, and share it.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and contact details when you subscribe or contact us
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our website through cookies and analytics
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, and operating system for technical optimization

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Send newsletters and updates (with your consent)
  • Analyze website usage to enhance user experience
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns
  • Deliver personalized content
  • Support our advertising partners (see below)

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect information:

  • Google Analytics: For website usage analysis
  • Advertising Networks: To display relevant ads
  • Email Services: For newsletter delivery

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your data.

Advertising

We use Google AdSense and other advertising networks to display ads on our site. These services may:

  • Use cookies to personalize ads based on your browsing history
  • Collect anonymous data about your interactions with ads
  • Employ interest-based advertising techniques

You can opt out of personalized advertising through Google's Ads Settings.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal data

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on our website with an updated revision date.
